5 Steps Filtration

our 5-step water filtration process ensures that every drop of water is pure, safe, and refreshing. The process begins with pre-filtration, which removes large particles like sand and dirt. Next, activated carbon filtration eliminates chlorine, bad odors, and harmful chemicals, improving taste and smell. We then use reverse osmosis (RO) to remove up to 99% of dissolved solids, bacteria, and heavy metals, followed by UV sterilization to kill any remaining microorganisms. Finally, post-filtration polishes the water, ensuring it is crystal clear and ready for consumption. With this thorough process, you can trust that your water is of the highest quality.

Composition

we use high-quality materials and advanced technology in the composition of our water purifiers to ensure exceptional performance and durability. Our purifiers include activated carbon filters to remove chlorine, chemicals, and odors; reverse osmosis membranes to eliminate heavy metals, bacteria, and viruses; UV sterilization units for neutralizing harmful microorganisms; sediment filters to capture dirt, sand, and rust; and food-grade plastics for safety and durability, ensuring no harmful substances leach into the water. Each component is carefully selected to provide you with the cleanest, safest water possible.
